# Nuclear Compliance - Approved Claims

**Purpose**: Document what sales, marketing, and executives can legally say about the Nuclear vertical.

**Audience**: Commercial team, executives, partners

**Last Reviewed**: 2026-01-25

---

## ✅ Approved Positioning Statement

> "RegEngine provides a compliance evidence layer that helps nuclear operators meet NRC recordkeeping and cybersecurity documentation obligations under 10 CFR. It operates out-of-band, preserves evidence immutably, and supports inspection and discovery."

**Why This Is Safe**:
- Claims are bounded and provable
- Does not overstate system capabilities
- Acknowledges system boundaries
- Aligns with regulatory expectations

## ✅ Approved Use Cases

### What Customers Use This For

**1. NRC Inspection Readiness**
- Cryptographically prove record integrity to inspectors
- Export evidence with verification metadata
- Demonstrate complete chain-of-custody

**2. Cybersecurity Program Documentation**
- Maintain immutable record of cyber security events (10 CFR 73.54)
- Preserve defensive architecture reviews
- Document incident response

**3. Legal Hold & Discovery**
- Preserve evidence during enforcement actions
- Prevent spoliation
- Export for legal discovery

**4. QA Program Support**
- Evidence layer for QA records (10 CFR 50 App B)
- Immutable document control
- Attribution and traceability

**5. Decommissioning Records**
- Retention for license life + 3 years
- Retrievable format (10 CFR 72.174)
- Transfer to successor licensees
