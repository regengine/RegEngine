## Key Assumptions

1. **Conversion rate:** 20-30% of design partners convert to paid annual (conservative)
2. **Sales cycle:** 2-4 weeks for SMB (self-serve), 6-12 weeks for mid-market (founder-led), 3-6 months for enterprise
3. **Expansion:** 20% of Year 1 customers upgrade tiers by Year 2 (Starter --> Growth)
4. **Supplier portal viral coefficient:** Each paying customer generates 5-15 supplier portal submissions/month; 5-10% of suppliers convert to own paid account within 6 months
5. **Churn:** Low due to regulatory mandate (compliance is non-discretionary); model assumes <10% annual logo churn
6. **Hiring:** First GTM hire (Technical Account Manager) at month 6; first AE at month 10; engineering grows to 3-4 by end of Year 1
