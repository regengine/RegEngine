## Outreach Targeting Criteria

### Tier 1 (Highest Priority)
- Produce or seafood suppliers shipping to Walmart/Sam's Club
- 1-10 facilities
- Currently sending EDI ASNs (or being forced to start)
- VP/Director of Food Safety or Supply Chain on LinkedIn

### Tier 2
- Dairy, deli/prepared foods, or shell egg suppliers to major retailers
- Distributors/3PLs managing mixed-lot pallets for FTL items
- Companies that attended Food Safety Summit or GS1 Connect

### Tier 3
- Food manufacturers with transformation events (co-mingling, re-labeling)
- Foreign exporters of FTL foods to US market
- Companies referenced in Reagan-Udall roundtable findings
