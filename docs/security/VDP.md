# Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

RegEngine welcomes reports of security vulnerabilities.

## Where to Report

Email: security@regengine.co

Include:
- Affected URL / endpoint
- Steps to reproduce
- Impact assessment
- Any supporting PoC (screenshots, logs, HTTP requests)

## Response SLA

| Stage           | Target             |
|-----------------|--------------------|
| Acknowledgment  | 2 business days    |
| Triage          | 5 business days    |
| Remediation     | 30 days (critical) |
|                 | 90 days (other)    |

We'll keep you informed of progress throughout remediation.

## Safe Harbor

If you act in good faith, avoid privacy violations, and avoid service
disruption, we will not pursue legal action for your report.

## Out of Scope

- Denial-of-service (DoS) testing
- Social engineering of staff or customers
- Physical attacks
- Vulnerabilities requiring MITM on user devices
- Automated scanning without prior coordination

## Guidelines

- Use test accounts and non-production data when possible
- Minimize data access and stop once impact is demonstrated
- Give us reasonable time to remediate before any public disclosure
- Coordinate disclosure timelines with us via the email above
