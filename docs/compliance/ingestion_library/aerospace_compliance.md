# ✈️ Aerospace Compliance – Audits & Certifications

## 1. Primary Regulatory Authorities

* Federal Aviation Administration (FAA)
* International Aerospace Quality Group (IAQG)
* National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA)
* U.S. Department of Defense (DoD)

## 2. Quality & Process Audits

* AS9100 / AS9101 audit checklists
* Auditor evaluation guidance documents
* Internal audit planning templates

## 3. Certification & Determination Documents

* FAA Type Certificate Data Sheets (TCDS)
* Airworthiness determinations
* Production approval documentation

## 4. Government Oversight Audits

* NASA Office of Inspector General audit reports
* DoD Inspector General contractor audits
* Government Accountability Office (GAO) aerospace reports

## 5. Enforcement & Risk Signals

* Certificate suspensions or findings
* Contractor non-conformance reports
* Quality escape investigations
