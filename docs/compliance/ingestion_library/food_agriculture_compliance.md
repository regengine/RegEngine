# 🥗 Food & Agriculture Compliance – Inspections & Audits

## 1. Primary Regulatory Authorities

* Food and Drug Administration (FDA)
* United States Department of Agriculture (USDA / FSIS)
* State Departments of Health

## 2. Government Inspection Documents

* FDA Form 483 inspection observations
* Establishment Inspection Reports (EIRs)
* Warning letters and close-out letters

## 3. FSMA & Preventive Controls

* Hazard Analysis & Preventive Controls documentation
* Food Safety Plan audit summaries
* Supply-chain verification records

## 4. Third-Party Food Safety Audits

* SQF audit reports (public samples)
* BRCGS certification audit summaries
* Corrective action and recertification reports

## 5. Enforcement & Risk Signals

* Product recalls
* Import alerts
* Suspension of registration
