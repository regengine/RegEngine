# 📘 Automotive Compliance – Public Audit & Oversight Documents

## 1. Primary Regulatory Authorities

* National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA)
* Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)
* International Automotive Task Force (IATF)
* SAE International

## 2. Audit & Investigation Documents (Public)

### Safety & Recalls

* NHTSA Office of Defects Investigation (ODI) reports
* Vehicle recall investigation summaries
* Manufacturer defect notifications

### Emissions & Environmental Compliance

* EPA vehicle certification data
* Emissions test group compliance reports
* Heavy-duty vehicle GHG compliance filings

## 3. Quality Management System Audits

* IATF 16949 audit checklists (supplier-level)
* Internal audit templates from TÜV, BSI, SGS
* Corrective action (CAR) templates

> Note: Full third-party audit reports are typically confidential; templates and checklists are the public proxy.

## 4. Enforcement & Risk Signals

* Recall enforcement actions
* Civil penalties related to emissions violations
* Consent decrees (e.g., emissions scandals)

## 5. Best Use for Analysis

* Control effectiveness benchmarking
* Supplier compliance pattern extraction
* Recall risk modeling
