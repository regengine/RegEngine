# ☢️ Nuclear Compliance – Safety & Operational Oversight

## 1. Primary Regulatory Authorities

* Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC)
* Department of Energy (DOE)
* Institute of Nuclear Power Operations (INPO)

## 2. Inspection & Event Documentation

* NRC inspection reports (reactors & facilities)
* Licensee Event Reports (LERs)
* Part 21 defect notifications

## 3. Operational & Safety Audits

* Radiation safety audit reports
* Emergency preparedness evaluations
* Security inspection summaries

## 4. Contractor & Laboratory Audits

* DOE Office of Inspector General audit reports
* National laboratory contractor assessments
* Nuclear procurement compliance audits

## 5. Enforcement & Risk Signals

* Notices of violation
* Reactor shutdown orders
* License amendments and conditions

> Note: INPO assessments are member-only; NRC and DOE documents are the public substitutes.
