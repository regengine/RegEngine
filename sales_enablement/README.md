## 🎯 Sales Playbook by Vertical

### Technology (SaaS/Startups)
**Primary Value**: Sales velocity (close deals 3 months faster)
**Secondary Value**: SOC 2/ISO 27001 cost reduction
**Objection**: "We're too small" → Response: "Growth tier is $120K, pays back in 3.3 months"
**Close**: "Every month you delay = 1 lost enterprise deal"

### Construction
**Primary Value**: Payment dispute avoidance ($300K/year litigation)
**Secondary Value**: OSHA fine reduction
**Objection**: "BIM 360 is cheaper" → Response: "But it doesn't prevent payment disputes (worth $2M)"
**Close**: "One major dispute costs more than 6 years of RegEngine"

### Healthcare
**Primary Value**: Breach cost avoidance ($10M avg breach)
**Secondary Value**: OCR investigation response time
**Objection**: "We've never had a breach" → Response: "91% of healthcare orgs had a breach in last 3 years"
**Close**: "Can you prove to OCR that records weren't tampered with?"

### Manufacturing (ISO)
**Primary Value**: Triple-cert efficiency (60% less duplicate documentation)
**Secondary Value**: CAPA closure rate improvement
**Objection**: "We already have a QMS" → Response: "Can it mathematically prove evidence integrity?"
**Close**: "Next ISO audit is when? You want this operational before then."

### Nuclear
**Primary Value**: License to operate (regulatory requirement)
**Secondary Value**: NRC inspection response time
**Objection**: "Too expensive" → Response: "What's the cost of a 1-month shutdown?" ($83M)
**Close**: "This isn't optional. NRC requires immutable safety records."
