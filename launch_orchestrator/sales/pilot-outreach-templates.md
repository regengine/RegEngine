## Usage Notes

- **Personalize the bracket fields** — company name, product category, and any specific detail you noticed on their LinkedIn or website.
- **LinkedIn message:** Send as a connection request note or DM. Keep it under 300 characters.
- **Cold email:** Best sent Tue–Thu, 8–10am in recipient's time zone.
- **Follow-up:** Send exactly 5 business days after the cold email. Same thread (reply to your original).
- **Target categories:** Leafy greens, fresh-cut fruits, certain cheeses, nut butters, fresh herbs, tropical tree fruits.
- **Target company size:** 10–50 employees. Look for quality/compliance managers, ops directors, or founders.
